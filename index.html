Things I want to learn
• How to build a product from start to finish
This is difficult and hard to know how to do right. It requires not only knowing about people and what they want, but having the fortitude to stick to your intuition about what they really need.
• How to build a product that people love using
I want to learn about how to build something simple enough to be useful and magical enough to be fun to use. I believe this requires a lot of humility.
• How to stay focused on one thing at a time
I do my best work when I'm not distracted, but when things aren't going smoothly it can be far too easy to shift focus. I want to hone my focus and work on worthwhile projects.
• How to accomplish long term goals
I'd like to find a way to pin down my heart's desires and accomplish them step by step, no matter how hard it is or how long it takes.
• How to love more fully
I would like to find the strength and freedom of mind to care about people even if they don't like me or help me or know me, and open myself up enough to even love them.

Skills
• Designing and building webapps
I've been building websites since 2008 and building webapps since 2010. I've learned how to use a ton of tools, become much more familiar with reading code and documentation, and developed good habits for making nice-looking, functional designs.
• Designing icons and buttons
I like adding small touches to a design that make a big difference to how it's experiences. Simple, clear imagery and simple, clear buttons are essential for this and I take pride in how I craft these.
• Writing stories
Stories are my favorite way of communicating complicated concepts or emotions. I think learning how to tell stories is essential for communicating well.
• Learning new things
I love learning new things and I set aside time regularly to learn new technologies or to figure out a better way of accomplishing a goal I've been working on.
• Prototyping ideas
Making things work is immensely satisfying, but being able to create a preview before you get to the hard part of building makes it so much easier to stay focused and build excitement around an idea. Prototyping a new idea is one of my favorite things to do.
