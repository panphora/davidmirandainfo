Things I want to learn
• How to build a product from scratch
I'd like to think that, with some practice, I can get better at staying focused on solving a single intractable problem until I can develop a solution.
• How to build a product that people love
Creating a tool that dares to be complicated enough to be incredibly simple to the point of seeming like magic sounds like a lot of fun to me.
• How to focus on only one thing at a time
I don't think there's a trick to this, but I know that when I hone my attention, I become the thing I'm working on and do my best work.
• How to accomplish long term goals
With time set aside every day, and forgiveness by my side, I hope to discover my deepest, most fulfilling dreams and try to bring them here.
• How to love more fully
It's difficult to say what I mean by this. Basically, what I mean is I think life is (for the most part) wonderful, and it deserves more grateful human hearts.
